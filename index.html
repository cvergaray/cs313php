About Me

I am a Computer Science Major currently in my 8th semester.
Creditwise, I am a senior, though I've only been in this program for two years now.
Thus, I still have three semesters left, plus my internship.
I have changed my major many times now and finally settled on Computer Science.
I was born in Renton, Washington and lived a large portion of my life in Kent, WA
before moving to Vernon, Connecticut.

When I am tempted to let a moment of my life pass unappreciated, I remember my favorite line from one of my favorite plays:

"Does anyone ever realize life while they live it... every, every minute?"
-Thornton Wilder, Our Town
